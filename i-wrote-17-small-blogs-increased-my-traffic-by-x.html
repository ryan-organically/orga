Starting from square 1

I've been doing SEO for about a year now. But my blog is still pretty new. I do web design too, and that takes some time away from blogging.

But I've neglected my blog for long enough. So I'm doing this study.

Here's my process:

I hit the keyword explorer to find some words with very low keyword difficulty (keywords that Ahrefs has determined do not require many backlinks to rank in the top results).

I'm in the search engine marketing/website design niche, so I'm searching for untapped keywords relating to topics surrounding web design and SEO.
